BRAND Reckonna VERSION 0.1 · concept PALETTE Forest Reserve

Everyday accounting, professionally managed.

Reckonna is a modern accounting product drawn with an accountant's hand. The system pairs a confident serif with a disciplined monospace, prefers paper-toned surfaces over chrome, and earns its color by speaking the language of debit and credit. This document is the canonical reference.

01

Identity

A calm, professional brand for people who want professional-standard accounting without needing professional-level knowledge.

Promise

Reckonna helps users manage everyday accounting with professional standards — through simple workflows, clear structure, and supportive guidance.

Personality

  • Calm, not cold
  • Professional, not intimidating
  • Modern, not trendy
  • Helpful, not overly technical
02

Logo

The mark is the balanced-columns lockup. Two columns stand for the T-account — the original gesture of double-entry bookkeeping — without naming it.

Reckonna
Reckonna
Reckonna
Clearspace

Reserve a clearspace equal to the height of one "R" cap on every side of the lockup. The mark never sits inside another shape; it is always the dominant element in its frame.

Don't

Don't recolor the columns, don't set the wordmark in monospace, don't outline or shadow the mark. For favicons, use the symbol alone at 16×16 minimum.

Pairing in body

Headings, sentences, and quoted notes go in serif. Numbers, IDs, account codes, ledger memos, navigation, and buttons go in mono. Italic serif (12–13px) is reserved for editorial subtitles and inline "Reckonna note" voice.

Sentence-shaped capture
On May 24, 2026 I paid $ 384.00 to Linear, Inc.

The defining anti-spreadsheet pattern: a fluent sentence whose blanks are also the form fields. Cursor moves left-to-right; meaning carries from the reading order; nothing about it suggests a grid.

08

Voice

Plain-language and reassuring. Clear, concise, operational. Never judgmental, never overly corporate.

Yes
  • "Your April books are ready to close."
  • "Reckonna matched this to a Mercury line, same day."
  • "Today's price is about 15% above fair value."
  • "Signals are model outputs, not advice."
No
  • "AUDIT REQUIRED" · audit-coded language
  • "You forgot to categorize 4 entries" · scolding
  • "Unlock premium analytics" · upsell jargon
  • Emoji or exclamation marks
